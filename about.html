About Me

Hello! I'm Royi Rozen, an XR Designer and Prototyper based in New York City. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and design, where I create immersive experiences that bridge the gap between digital and physical worlds.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Computer and Information Sciences at Cornell Tech, where I'm expanding my knowledge in emerging technologies and human-computer interaction. My academic focus includes spatial computing, extended reality, and interactive media design.

Prior to my graduate studies, I founded ARoundU, an XR studio focused on creating transformative digital experiences for clients across various industries. Our projects range from educational AR applications to immersive VR training simulations and interactive media installations.

I believe that extended reality technologies have the power to reshape how we interact with information, spaces, and each other. My work aims to create meaningful connections through technology that enhances rather than replaces human experience.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me exploring New York City's art scene, hiking in upstate New York, or experimenting with new cooking recipes. I'm always open to collaborations and exciting opportunities, so feel free to reach out!
