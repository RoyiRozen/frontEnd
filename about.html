About Me

Hi! I'm Royi Rozen, an XR Designer, Product Leader, and Innovator based in New York City. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and design, where I create immersive XR experiences that address real-world challenges and bridge the gap between digital and physical spaces.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's in Computer & Information Sciences at Cornell Tech, specializing in Computer Vision, Extended Reality (XR), and Health Tech. My academic and professional work includes integrating Augmented Reality into healthtech applications.

As the founder of ARoundU, an XR studio, I lead projects ranging from educational AR experiences and immersive VR training simulations to interactive media installations. I truly believe that XR and smart-glasses are about to reshape our interactions, enhance human experiences, and solve significant challenges.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me exploring New York City's diverse communities, hiking upstate, or diving into a great book. I'm always open to new collaborations and exciting opportunities - feel free to connect!
