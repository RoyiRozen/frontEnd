About

Royi Rozen | Product Leader, XR Builder & Innovator based in New York City. Currently pursuing an MS in Computer & Information Sciences at Cornell Tech, Royi specializes in designing, developing, and leading XR experiences that leverage technology to tackle pressing real-world challenges. He is the founder of ARoundU, an XR studio dedicated to creating transformative digital experiences, and is actively working in research implementing Augmented Reality in healthtech settings. Royi is open to exciting collaborations and opportunities throughout the summer.
