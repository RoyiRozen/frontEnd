ARTour

AR Product UI/UX Dev Spatial Design Creative 3D

AR Tour is a standalone augmented reality geolocation application designed to immerse users in historical storytelling by overlaying historical images at their original locations. It bridges the past and present, offering users a time-travel-like experience of exploring cultural landmarks and significant events.

Key Features

  • Temporal Overlay: Historical images aligned with present-day locations
  • Narrative Visualization: Recreating significant historical events where they occurred
  • Guided Discovery: Curated paths connecting related stories for deeper exploration
  • Audio Narration: Immersive storytelling with professional voice narration

UI Design Screens

AR Tour's interface is designed to be minimalist yet intuitive, allowing the historical content to take center stage while providing users with easy navigation and exploration tools.

Technical Approach

ARTour leverages Unity and AR Foundation for cross-platform development, integrating Google Geospatial API for precise location tracking and historical image alignment. The development process involved extensive historical research, geospatial data mapping, and iterative on-location testing to ensure accurate overlay positioning. Firebase handles user data and content management, while ElevenLabs provides AI-generated voice narration for immersive storytelling experiences.
