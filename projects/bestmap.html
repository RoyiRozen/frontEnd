BestMap NYC

Project Overview

BestMap NYC is an augmented reality application that transforms how tourists and locals navigate New York City. Utilizing spatial mapping and computer vision technologies, the app overlays relevant information, historical context, and personalized recommendations onto the physical landscape of the city.

The Challenge

Traditional maps and navigation apps often lack context and personalization, reducing cities to simple grid systems. This creates a disconnect between the rich cultural history of urban spaces and how people experience them. BestMap NYC was designed to bridge this gap by creating a more immersive and contextualized navigation experience.

Solution

By combining AR, machine learning, and user-generated content, BestMap NYC allows users to:

  • See historical overlays showing how neighborhoods have changed over time
  • Discover hidden gems and local recommendations based on their interests
  • Navigate using visual AR cues rather than traditional map interfaces
  • Learn about architectural features and historical significance of buildings
  • Access crowd-sourced stories and memories associated with specific locations

Technology Stack

BestMap NYC was built using:

  • Unity for AR development
  • ARKit and ARCore for spatial mapping
  • Custom computer vision algorithms for landmark recognition
  • Firebase for backend services and user-generated content
  • Historical GIS data integration from NYC archives
