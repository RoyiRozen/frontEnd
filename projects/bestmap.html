Project Brief

BestMap NYC is an augmented reality application that transforms how tourists and locals navigate New York City. Utilizing spatial mapping and computer vision technologies, the app overlays relevant information, historical context, and personalized recommendations onto the physical landscape of the city. My role involved [User to specify role, e.g., concept design, lead development, and UI/UX].

The Challenge & Our Solution

Traditional maps often lack context, reducing cities to simple grids. BestMap NYC bridges this gap by creating an immersive navigation experience. Users can see historical overlays, discover local recommendations via AR cues, learn about architecture, and access crowd-sourced stories.

Key Features

  • Historical overlays showing neighborhood changes.
  • Personalized local recommendations via AR.
  • Visual AR navigation cues.
  • Information on architectural and historical building significance.
  • Crowd-sourced stories and memories for locations.

Technology Insights

BestMap NYC was built using Unity for AR development, ARKit/ARCore for spatial mapping, custom computer vision algorithms for landmark recognition, Firebase for backend services, and integrated historical GIS data from NYC archives.
