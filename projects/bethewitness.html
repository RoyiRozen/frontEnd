Be the Witness VR Documentary

2023
VR 3D Video Documentary

Project Brief

Be the Witness is a Virtual Reality documentary sharing stories from October 7th, 2023, when Hamas attacked Israel. Led by the Steven Spielberg Foundation, my role focused on UX/UI spatial design for the virtual environment. The core challenge was to balance immersive storytelling with the ethical need for a safe viewing experience when confronting traumatic narratives, giving the viewer control and agency.

Design Philosophy & Approach

My design approach centered on creating a virtual environment that honors powerful testimonies while giving viewers agency. This involved an abstract representation of actual environments using Gaussian splat rendering, balancing the reality of events with an aesthetic distance to prevent overwhelming the viewer. The initial concept presentation was crucial for aligning stakeholders.

The testimonies shared in this documentary are powerful and deeply personal. My design approach centered on creating a virtual environment that honors these stories while giving viewers agency over their experience.

View Presentation

Technical & Process Highlights

Key technologies included VR development (Unity), Gaussian Splat rendering for environmental abstraction, and careful UX/UI design for emotional safety. The process involved stakeholder alignment, iterative spatial UX design focusing on viewer control, environment rendering, and user testing to ensure an impactful yet respectful experience.

Impact & Significance

"Be the Witness" pioneers a responsible approach to VR documentary for sensitive topics. It shows how immersive tech can share vital testimonies while respecting subjects and viewers, establishing new paths for ethical immersive journalism. The abstract visuals also suggest new aesthetic directions for VR documentaries that balance authenticity with viewer well-being.
