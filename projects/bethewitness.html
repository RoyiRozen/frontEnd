Project Overview

Be the Witness is a Virtual Reality documentary project that shares the stories of October 7th, 2023, when Hamas attacked Israel. This immersive experience was led by the Steven Spielberg Foundation, with my contribution focused on UX/UI spatial design for the virtual environment.

Abstract

Being present for horrific stories is an intensive experience that must be dealt with care. I envisioned a place that gives control to the viewer - a safe space is needed when confronting traumatic narratives. This project balances the power of immersive storytelling with the ethical responsibility to protect viewers from potential retraumatization.

The testimonies shared in this documentary are powerful and deeply personal. My design approach centered on creating a virtual environment that honors these stories while giving viewers agency over their experience.

Design Approach

This presentation was the first step of my design process on this project, ensuring concept alignment between all of the numerous stakeholders involved. My approach focused on creating an abstract representation of the actual environment rather than a realistic recreation of traumatic scenes.

I knew I wanted to use an abstract representation of the actual environment, hence I rendered a complete 360-degree environment as a Gaussian splat, helping to create this unique look and feel. This technique allowed us to present the reality of events while maintaining a necessary aesthetic distance that prevents overwhelming the viewer.

The implementation featured a carefully designed spatial interface that allows viewers to navigate through testimonies at their own pace. Design Process

1
Stakeholder Alignment

Created preliminary concepts and presentations to align vision across multiple stakeholders, including the Steven Spielberg Foundation representatives.

2
Spatial UX Design

Developed spatial interfaces that give viewers control over their experience, creating a safe environment for engaging with difficult testimonies.

3
Environment Rendering

Utilized Gaussian splat techniques to create abstract representations of environments, balancing visual context with ethical considerations.

4
User Testing & Refinement

Conducted iterative testing to ensure the experience was both impactful and respectful, with a focus on giving viewers agency within the documentary format.

Impact & Significance

"Be the Witness" represents an innovative approach to documentary storytelling in virtual reality, particularly for sensitive subject matter. The project demonstrates how immersive technologies can be used responsibly to share important testimonies while respecting both the subjects and viewers.

By creating a spatially designed environment that gives viewers control over their experience, the project establishes new possibilities for ethical immersive journalism and documentary filmmaking. The abstract visual approach using Gaussian splat techniques also points toward new aesthetic directions for documentary VR that balance authenticity with viewer well-being.
