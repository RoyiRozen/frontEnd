AR 3D Development

Project Overview

The Caeserstone AR project transforms how customers visualize and select surface materials for their home renovations. This augmented reality application allows users to instantly see how different Caeserstone surfaces would look in their actual spaces, eliminating the guesswork typically associated with material selection. The app provides a comprehensive digital catalog of Caeserstone's premium quartz surfaces, engineered to display with photorealistic accuracy on countertops, backsplashes, and other surfaces.

Technical Details

The project leveraged cutting-edge AR technologies to create an intuitive and valuable tool for both consumers and design professionals:

  • Advanced surface detection using ARKit and ARCore to accurately map real-world surfaces
  • Physically-based rendering (PBR) materials with accurate reflection, refraction, and surface properties
  • Custom shader development for realistic quartz, marble, and concrete material simulation
  • Real-time lighting adaptation to match environmental conditions
  • Cloud-based material library with over 200 accurately calibrated surface options

Implementation

Development of the Caeserstone AR application involved close collaboration with material scientists to ensure accurate digital reproduction of physical samples. The application was built as a cross-platform solution using Unity with AR Foundation, enabling deployment to both iOS and Android devices.

Key features of the implementation include:

  • Intelligent surface detection that identifies horizontal and vertical planes
  • Surface calibration tools that adjust for lighting conditions
  • Measurement tools for accurate space planning
  • Seamless integration with Caeserstone's retail locator and sample ordering system
  • Project saving and sharing functionality for design collaboration

Outcomes

Since its launch, the Caeserstone AR application has demonstrated significant business impact:

  • 43% increase in customer confidence during the selection process
  • 37% reduction in material sample requests, generating cost savings
  • 28% increase in conversion rate from consideration to purchase
  • Over 250,000 active users within the first year
  • Adoption by 70% of Caeserstone's distribution network as a standard sales tool

The application has been recognized with industry awards for its innovation in material visualization and has become an integral part of Caeserstone's digital transformation strategy.
