Project Overview

Dot Space AI redefines collaborative interior design by leveraging generative artificial intelligence to transform the way professionals and clients work together on spatial planning projects. The platform uses AI to generate contextually relevant design options based on user preferences, space constraints, and style requirements, significantly reducing the iteration time between concept and implementation.

Technical Details

The project leverages several advanced technologies to deliver a seamless design experience:

  • Machine learning models trained on thousands of interior designs and spatial arrangements
  • Real-time 3D visualization using WebGL for immediate feedback on design changes
  • Multi-user collaboration infrastructure with conflict resolution
  • Style transfer algorithms to adapt designs to specific aesthetic preferences
  • Integration with furniture and material databases for realistic rendering and procurement

Implementation

Leading the development of Dot Space AI involved coordinating cross-functional teams of designers, engineers, and AI specialists. We implemented an agile methodology with two-week sprints, focusing on continuous user testing and iterative improvements. The platform was built as a progressive web application, ensuring accessibility across devices while maintaining the performance necessary for complex visualizations.

A significant challenge was creating AI models that could generate designs that were both innovative and practical. This required extensive training on both successful designs and practical constraints such as building codes, traffic patterns, and material limitations. We developed a proprietary feedback loop system that allowed the AI to learn from designer corrections, continuously improving its suggestions.
