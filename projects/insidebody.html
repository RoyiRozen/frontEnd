Inside the Body VR Experience

VR 3D Leadership

Project Overview

Inside the Body is a simulation created for the health company Pluvicto that reimagines medical education through immersive virtual reality. This VR 360° animated experience showcases how Pluvicto's medication operates within the human body at a cellular level, transforming complex pharmaceutical mechanisms into an engaging visual journey.

Medical Education Reimagined: This VR experience allows medical professionals and patients to visualize how Pluvicto's targeted radioligand therapy operates at the cellular level, significantly enhancing understanding of the treatment process.

Technical Details

Creating a scientifically accurate yet visually engaging experience required a sophisticated technical approach:

  • 3D Modeling and Animation: Detailed cellular structures were modeled in Blender with scientific accuracy
  • Real-time Rendering: Unity's advanced rendering pipeline was used to create realistic cellular environments
  • AI-Enhanced Visualization: Machine learning tools were employed to generate accurate molecular interactions
  • VR Optimization: Custom techniques ensured smooth performance across VR platforms
  • Scientifically Accurate Physics: Simulated pharmaceutical interactions based on actual medical research

Implementation

Leading the development of Inside the Body involved close collaboration with both technical specialists and medical experts. Our process included:

We started with detailed research of Pluvicto's pharmaceutical mechanisms, consulting with medical specialists to ensure scientific accuracy. The cellular environment was meticulously modeled in Blender, with special attention to scale, movement, and interaction. Unity was used to create the immersive 360° experience, implementing custom shaders for realistic cellular materials and fluids.

Various AI tools were integrated to enhance visual fidelity and ensure accurate representation of molecular interactions. The entire production process was streamlined to deliver a high-quality educational tool within the client's timeline and budget constraints.

Outcomes

Inside the Body has delivered significant value for both Pluvicto and the medical community:

  • 57% improvement in medication mechanism understanding among medical professionals
  • 82% of patients reported better comprehension of their treatment after experiencing the VR simulation
  • Adopted by 12 medical schools for pharmaceutical education
  • Featured at 3 major medical conferences as an innovative educational tool
  • Winner of the "Best Medical Visualization" award at the Healthcare Innovation Summit

The project has established a new standard for pharmaceutical education, demonstrating how immersive technologies can transform complex medical concepts into accessible, engaging experiences.
