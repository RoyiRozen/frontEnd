MedComm

Visit MedComm Live

MedComm is an AI-powered medical communication training platform designed to prepare emergency medicine residents for the 2025 ABEM certification requirements. The system provides scalable, standardized training in crucial communication skills through realistic simulated scenarios with patients, families, and healthcare providers.

Challenge & Solution

Emergency medicine residents face significant challenges preparing for the revised 2025 ABEM certification examination, which now requires demonstration of communication skills within realistic simulated scenarios. Traditional role-playing training is resource-intensive, difficult to standardize, and not scalable for large programs.

MedComm addresses this through an AI-powered chatbot simulation system that generates realistic interactions based on ABEM certification scenarios. The platform provides on-demand practice opportunities with consistent, high-quality training that can scale to serve numerous residents simultaneously.

Key Features

  • ABEM-Aligned Scenarios: 100+ unique scenarios focusing on difficult conversations, patient-centered decision-making, and conflict management
  • Methodological Learning Framework: Structured conversational paths with adaptive feedback for establishing rapport and empathetic communication
  • Realistic Dialogue Engine: Advanced NLP and LLMs (GPT-3.5/GPT-4) for dynamic, context-aware conversations with voice input/output
  • Performance Analytics Dashboard: Tracks resident progress, identifies improvement areas, and provides insights into communication patterns

User Interface

The platform features an intuitive interface designed for both residents and administrators, with clear visual cues and interactive elements that guide users through simulations and feedback processes.
