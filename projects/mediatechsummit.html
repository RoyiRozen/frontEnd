A Resounding Success: The Inaugural MediaTech Summit

The first annual Cornell Tech MediaTech Summit, held in April 2025, was a landmark event, bringing together over 200+ students, faculty, industry leaders, and tech enthusiasts. The summit fostered vibrant discussions and networking, exploring the cutting-edge intersection of media, technology, and innovation. We are thrilled with the engagement and insights shared, setting a strong foundation for future MediaTech endeavors at Cornell Tech.

Date & Time: April 2025

Location: Verizon Executive Education Center, Cornell Tech, NYC

Attendees: 200+ (Students, Faculty, Industry Leaders, Tech Enthusiasts)

Core Focus: Exploring the dynamic intersection of media and technology, fostering innovation and collaboration.

Event Highlights in Pictures

My Role & Contributions

  • Strategic Planning: Defined summit theme, objectives, and program structure with faculty teams
  • Speaker Coordination: Curated and managed industry leaders and academic experts as panelists
  • Panel Development: Led planning and moderation of key discussion sessions
  • Marketing & Outreach: Attracted 200+ attendees from Cornell Tech and NYC tech community
  • Event Operations: Managed on-site logistics for seamless attendee experience

Impact and Outcomes

  • Knowledge Sharing: Deep dives into AI content creation, AR/VR futures, digital trust, and MediaTech business models
  • Networking: Connected 200+ students with industry professionals, fostering new collaborations
  • Innovation Showcase: Platform for Cornell Tech research and cutting-edge project demonstrations
  • Community Building: Established MediaTech Summit as Cornell Tech's key annual industry event
"The MediaTech Summit 2025 successfully bridged the gap between academic exploration and industry application, sparking collaborations that will undoubtedly shape the future of media."
