MemoriAR

AR Creative Product

MemoriAR is a location-based augmented reality memorial platform created in response to the October 7th, 2023 tragedy. This meaningful application enables families to preserve and honor memories of loved ones through persistent AR experiences, allowing visitors to encounter digital memorials at specific physical locations using mobile devices.

Digital Memorial Concept

Traditional memorials are limited by physical space and accessibility, often requiring visitors to travel to specific locations that may not hold personal significance. MemoriAR reimagines memorial experiences by allowing families to create persistent, location-based augmented reality tributes that can be placed at meaningful locations - a favorite park, family home, or place of significance. These digital memorials include photos, videos, 3D objects, recorded stories, and interactive elements that preserve not just memories but the essence of a person's life, creating lasting connections between physical spaces and cherished memories.

Community Healing

MemoriAR serves as both a personal healing tool and a community platform for collective remembrance. Visitors can leave messages, virtual flowers, or tokens of remembrance that become part of the memorial experience, creating a shared space for grief and healing. The platform enables families to invite others to contribute memories and stories, building comprehensive digital archives that capture the full impact of a person's life. This collaborative approach transforms individual loss into community support, providing comfort through shared remembrance and ensuring that important stories are preserved for future generations.

Technical Innovation

Built using Unity with Google Geospatial API for precise location anchoring, MemoriAR ensures memorial experiences remain exactly where families place them, accessible to anyone with the application. The platform uses advanced AR tracking to maintain stable placement of digital content in real-world environments, while cloud-based storage ensures memorials persist indefinitely. The application was recognized at the Google Geospatial Creator Hackathon for its innovative approach to memory preservation and sensitive response to real-world tragedy, demonstrating how AR technology can serve meaningful purposes beyond entertainment and commerce.
