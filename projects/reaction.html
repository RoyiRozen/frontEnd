Project Overview

Reactions is an augmented reality experience that transforms the Cornell Tech campus into an interactive social playground. Inspired by the overwhelming feeling of the first day of school, the project allows students and visitors to share emotions through AR emojis placed at specific locations around campus.

The first day of school can be overwhelming. Exploring campus for the first time and meeting a bunch of new people. Being passionate about AR, it felt like an opportunity to connect through shared emotions in physical spaces.

The Inspiration

Starting at a new school can be both exciting and intimidating. As new students explore the Cornell Tech campus, they encounter numerous locations, each with its own purpose and atmosphere. This sparked the idea for Reactions - a way to bridge the gap between physical spaces and emotional responses using augmented reality.

By placing virtual emoji reactions at specific locations, the project creates a shared emotional map of campus. New students can see how others have responded to different spaces, creating an instant connection through shared experiences. It's a digital layer of emotional context on top of the physical environment.

Technical Implementation

Reactions leverages cutting-edge AR technology to create a seamless experience:

Google AR & VR Geospatial Creator
Unity AR Foundation
GPS Integration
C# Programming

The project uses Google AR & VR Geospatial Creator to precisely position virtual content in real-world coordinates. This allows emoji reactions to remain persistently in their designated locations, visible to all users of the application. Unity's AR Foundation framework provides the cross-platform development environment, ensuring compatibility with both iOS and Android devices.

Key technical features include:

  • Accurate geospatial positioning to anchor virtual content to real-world locations
  • Cloud-based persistence of emoji placements for shared experiences
  • User-friendly interface for viewing and placing reactions
  • Optimization for mobile devices to ensure smooth performance
  • Location-based notifications to alert users of nearby reactions

Social Impact

Reactions showcases AR's potential to foster real-world social connections in several ways:

  • Breaking the Ice: Provides an easy conversation starter for new students exploring campus
  • Shared Experience: Creates a sense of community through collectively mapped emotional responses
  • Campus Discovery: Encourages exploration of the entire campus through distributed emoji locations
  • Digital Expression: Allows for emotional expression in physical spaces without permanent alteration
  • Evolving History: Creates a living record of how spaces make people feel over time

Unlike many AR experiences that isolate users in their own digital world, Reactions is designed to complement and enhance real-world social interactions. The AR layer serves as a conversation starter and shared reference point, encouraging face-to-face discussions about campus experiences.

Future Directions

While currently implemented on Cornell Tech's campus, Reactions has potential applications in many other contexts:

  • Other university and school campuses to help new students navigate social and academic spaces
  • Tourist destinations to share visitor experiences and highlight points of interest
  • Urban neighborhoods to create community-driven emotional maps of public spaces
  • Corporate campuses to improve employee orientation and workplace satisfaction
  • Museums and cultural institutions to capture visitor responses to exhibits and artworks

Future development will focus on expanding the range of possible reactions, improving the social sharing capabilities, and creating more robust analytics to understand how people emotionally respond to different spaces. The ultimate goal is to create a platform that helps build stronger connections between people and the places they inhabit.
