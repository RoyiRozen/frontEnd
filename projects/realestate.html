Project Overview

Real Estate Viz is a geolocation-based augmented reality experience that envisions a future where AR serves as a powerful tool for visualizing real estate development projects on-site. The application aims to streamline communication between developers and the public, providing an intuitive way to experience proposed buildings in their actual planned location before construction begins.

The gap between architectural renderings and the public's understanding of new developments has always been problematic. By bringing proposed buildings into the real world through AR, we can democratize the visualization process and foster more meaningful dialogue about urban development.

The Problem

Urban development projects often face challenges in effectively communicating their vision to various stakeholders:

  • Traditional architectural renderings can be misleading or difficult for non-professionals to interpret
  • Community members struggle to visualize how new developments will impact their neighborhoods
  • Planning boards and city officials need accurate context for decision-making
  • Physical models are expensive and can't be easily transported or viewed in-context
  • Development sites often sit empty for months or years, creating information gaps about future plans

These communication barriers can lead to misinformation, community opposition, and prolonged approval processes that increase costs and delays for developers.

AR Solution

Real Estate Viz addresses these challenges by leveraging cutting-edge AR technology to place accurate 3D models of proposed buildings directly on their future construction sites. This creates a shared visual language that bridges the gap between developers and the community.

Precise Geolocation

Technical Implementation

AR Foundation

The application was built using Unity with AR Foundation to ensure cross-platform compatibility. It incorporates several specialized technologies:

  • Hybrid Positioning System: Combines GPS data with visual positioning anchors for centimeter-level accuracy even in urban environments with GPS challenges
  • BIM Model Optimization: Custom pipeline that converts detailed architectural models (BIM) into optimized AR-ready assets that can run smoothly on mobile devices
  • Real-time Occlusion: Advanced depth sensing to ensure virtual buildings properly interact with existing structures and terrain
  • Environment-Aware Rendering: Lighting system that samples real-world conditions to create realistic shadows and reflections

Key Benefits

The Real Estate Viz platform offers significant advantages to multiple stakeholders:

  • For Developers
    Improve community relations, streamline approval processes, and market properties more effectively with immersive visualizations that can be experienced on-site.
  • For Community Members
    Gain a clear understanding of how new developments will impact neighborhood aesthetics, views, sunlight, and overall character before construction begins.
  • For City Planners
    Make more informed decisions by seeing proposed buildings in context, evaluating their relationship to existing urban fabric and master plans.
  • For Potential Buyers/Tenants
    Experience future properties in their actual location, understanding views, surroundings, and neighborhood context before making purchasing decisions.

User Experience Design

The application was designed with accessibility and ease-of-use as primary considerations, making architectural visualization available to everyone regardless of technical background:

  • Intuitive Interface: Simple controls for navigating 3D space and accessing information about the development
  • Multi-Modal Interaction: Options for touch controls, voice commands, and gesture recognition
  • Progressive Disclosure: Information presented in layers, from basic overview to detailed specifications
  • Guided Tours: Pre-programmed viewpoints highlighting key features and perspectives
  • Accessible Design: High-contrast modes, screen reader compatibility, and other accessibility features

The experience is designed to work equally well for individual users exploring on their own and for guided group presentations led by developers or planners.

Future Development

As Real Estate Viz evolves, several exciting enhancements are planned:

  • Integration with city-wide digital twins for broader urban context visualization
  • Simulation of traffic patterns and pedestrian flows around proposed developments
  • Environmental impact analysis showing factors like solar gain, wind patterns, and stormwater management
  • AR collaboration tools allowing multiple stakeholders to view and discuss projects simultaneously
  • Integration with VR to allow remote viewing of projects for those who cannot visit the physical site

The ultimate vision is to create a comprehensive platform that transforms how urban development is communicated, evaluated, and experienced by all stakeholders, leading to better-designed buildings and more inclusive decision-making processes.
