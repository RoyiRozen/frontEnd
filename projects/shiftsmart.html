ShiftSmart.AI

AI Product UI/UX Healthcare Data Science

Overview

ShiftSmart.AI is an AI-powered platform that optimizes emergency department staffing to reduce wait times, improve Medicare scores, and save on labor costs. Our solution helps hospitals align staff with real-time patient demand while maintaining CMS compliance.

Key Features

AI-Powered Predictions

Forecasts patient volumes and acuity with high accuracy (R² up to 0.97) using advanced machine learning models.

Real-time Analytics

Tracks key metrics including arrival-to-provider time and left without being seen rates in real-time.

Seamless Integration

Works alongside existing EHR systems like Epic and Cerner without disrupting clinical workflows.

Compliant & Secure

HIPAA-compliant data handling and processing with enterprise-grade security measures.
