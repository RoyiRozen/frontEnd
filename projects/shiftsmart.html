ShiftSmart AI Model: KPI Prediction

Synthetic Data

Arrival to Provider Time - Time from patient arrival to being seen.

Left Without Being Seen (%) - Indicates overcrowding or delays.

SEP-1 Compliance (%) - Sepsis treatment adherence.

Readmissions (30-day) - Signals care quality and cost risk.

We created a synthetic dataset on ChatGPT by using Government CMS hospitals data - we aimed to simulate emergency department operations across multiple hospitals. The data aggregation level is hour and date - and it includes patient arrivals, staffing, and key performance metrics like wait times and readmissions- to Signals care quality and cost risk. We then enriched it with operational constraints such as ICU capacity, equipment downtime, and SEP-1 compliance (Sepsis treatment adherence) to better reflect real-world complexity. This structure allows us to train and test machine learning models for staffing predictions and quality outcome forecasting.
